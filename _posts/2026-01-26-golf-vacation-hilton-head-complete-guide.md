Hilton Head Island has earned its reputation as one of America's premier golf destinations, offering more than 26 world-class courses amid 12 miles of pristine beaches and stunning Lowcountry scenery. Whether you are planning a buddies trip, couples getaway, or family vacation with golf, this comprehensive guide will help you create the perfect Hilton Head golf experience.

## Why Choose Hilton Head for Your Golf Vacation?

Hilton Head offers an unmatched combination of factors that make it ideal for golf travelers:

- **World-Class Courses:** Over 26 championship layouts designed by Pete Dye, Jack Nicklaus, Robert Trent Jones Sr., Rees Jones, and other legends
- **Perfect Weather:** Mild temperatures allow year-round play, with spring and fall offering ideal conditions
- **PGA TOUR Heritage:** Home to the RBC Heritage at Harbour Town Golf Links since 1969
- **Beach Access:** 12 miles of pristine Atlantic beaches for post-round relaxation
- **Southern Hospitality:** Exceptional dining, shopping, and entertainment
- **Easy Access:** Savannah/Hilton Head International Airport is just 45 miles away

## Best Time to Visit

### Peak Golf Season (March-May)
- Perfect weather with temperatures in the 70s
- Courses in prime condition
- RBC Heritage tournament week (mid-April)
- Higher prices and advance booking essential

### Fall Season (September-November)
- Excellent weather, slightly less crowded than spring
- Courses recovering from summer stress
- Great values on packages
- Hurricane season consideration

### Summer (June-August)
- Hot and humid conditions
- Best rates and availability
- Early morning and late afternoon tee times recommended
- Family vacation season

### Winter (December-February)
- Mild temperatures (50s-60s)
- Excellent values
- Some course maintenance periods
- Quieter atmosphere

## Planning Your Golf Itinerary

### The Classic 4-Day Golf Trip

**Day 1: Arrival Day**
- Morning flight arrival
- Play Hilton Head National (convenient location near bridges)
- Settle into accommodations
- Casual dinner at a local seafood spot

**Day 2: Resort Experience**
- Morning round at Harbour Town Golf Links
- Lunch at the Sea Pines clubhouse
- Afternoon at the beach or pool
- Upscale dinner in Harbour Town

**Day 3: Course Variety**
- Morning round at Oyster Reef Golf Club
- Quick lunch
- Afternoon round at Old South Golf Links
- Evening on the town in Coligny Plaza

**Day 4: Final Round**
- Robert Trent Jones Course at Palmetto Dunes
- Brunch before departure
- Afternoon flight home

### Extended 7-Day Itinerary

For a longer stay, add these courses to your rotation:
- Atlantic Dunes at Sea Pines
- George Fazio Course at Palmetto Dunes
- Arthur Hills Course at Palmetto Dunes
- Golden Bear at Indigo Run
- Include rest days with beach time, fishing, or spa visits

## Golf Packages and Booking

### Resort Stay-and-Play Packages

**Palmetto Dunes Oceanfront Resort**
Typical packages include:
- 4 days/3 nights in a 2-bedroom villa
- Two or three rounds on your choice of three championship courses
- Green fees and cart fees included
- One hour Toptracer experience
- Fully customizable with accommodations and activities

**Group Packages (12+ players)**
The Premier Package features outstanding resort accommodations plus multiple rounds on the George Fazio, Arthur Hills, or Robert Trent Jones courses.

### Independent Booking Tips

- **Book Early:** Confirm tee times up to 6 months in advance through local booking services
- **Compare Options:** Package providers with 20+ years of experience offer over 25 courses
- **Check Replay Rates:** Many courses offer discounted afternoon and replay rates
- **Consider Weekdays:** Better availability and often lower prices

### Club Rental Services

Skip the hassle of traveling with clubs. Local rental services offer:
- Free delivery and pickup at your accommodation
- Quality club sets for all rounds
- Savings versus course rentals ($85/day typical course rate)
- No airline baggage fees or damage risk

## Accommodations

### Vacation Rentals
Private vacation rentals offer the best value and convenience for golf groups:
- Full kitchens for breakfast and casual meals
- Multiple bedrooms for groups
- Living space for evening gatherings
- Often more space than hotel rooms at comparable prices
- Ideal for groups of 4-8 golfers

### Resort Options

**Sea Pines Resort**
- Oldest resort on Hilton Head (5,000 acres)
- Home to Harbour Town Golf Links
- Premium accommodations at Inn & Club at Harbour Town
- Direct access to Atlantic Dunes and Heron Point courses

**Palmetto Dunes Oceanfront Resort**
- Full-service marina and tennis center
- Villa and house accommodations
- Three championship courses on property
- Extensive amenities

## Budget Planning

### Sample Daily Budget (Per Person)

**Budget-Friendly:**
- Golf (value course): $80-$110
- Meals: $50-$75
- Accommodation share: $75-$100
- **Daily Total:** $200-$285

**Mid-Range:**
- Golf (quality resort course): $130-$200
- Meals: $75-$125
- Accommodation share: $100-$150
- **Daily Total:** $305-$475

**Premium:**
- Golf (Harbour Town/Atlantic Dunes): $250-$450
- Meals: $150-$200
- Accommodation share: $200-$300
- **Daily Total:** $600-$950

### Money-Saving Tips
- Play afternoon twilight rates
- Book stay-and-play packages
- Rent clubs locally instead of paying course fees
- Cook breakfast at your rental
- Look for shoulder season deals

## Getting There and Around

### Air Travel
**Savannah/Hilton Head International Airport (SAV)**
- 45 miles south of Hilton Head Island
- Many non-stop flights from major U.S. cities
- Rental cars readily available

### Ground Transportation
- Rental car recommended for flexibility
- Uber and Lyft available on the island
- Some resorts offer shuttle services

### On-Island Navigation
- The island is approximately 12 miles long
- Most courses are 10-20 minutes apart
- Traffic can be heavy during peak season, especially on weekends
