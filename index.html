Hilton Head Beach Area Guide

Discover Hilton Head Island

Hilton Head Island is a 12-mile barrier island off the coast of South Carolina, renowned for its pristine beaches, world-class golf, and relaxed Lowcountry charm. Our Hilton Head beach house rental puts you right in the heart of the Folly Field area, one of the island's most sought-after neighborhoods for visitors seeking a quiet, family-friendly beach experience.

About Folly Field Beach

Folly Field Beach is one of Hilton Head's best-kept secrets. Unlike the busier Coligny Beach area, Folly Field offers wide, uncrowded sandy shores with calm, shallow waters ideal for families with young children. The beach features public restrooms, outdoor showers, seasonal lifeguards from Memorial Day through Labor Day, and beach rental equipment including chairs, umbrellas, and boogie boards. Sunrise views over the Atlantic are spectacular from this east-facing stretch of coastline.

What's Nearby

From our luxury beach house on Hilton Head Island, you're minutes away from everything the island has to offer. Coligny Plaza, the island's most popular shopping and dining district, is a short drive or bike ride away. Shelter Cove Harbour offers waterfront dining with stunning sunset views. For nature lovers, the Coastal Discovery Museum and Pinckney Island National Wildlife Refuge provide opportunities to explore the island's rich maritime ecosystem, while 60+ miles of paved bike paths make Hilton Head one of America's most bike-friendly destinations.

Dining and Restaurants

Hilton Head Island's dining scene ranges from casual beachside eateries to award-winning fine dining. Within minutes of our Hilton Head private rental, you'll find fresh seafood at local favorites, Lowcountry cuisine featuring shrimp and grits, and waterfront restaurants perfect for a sunset dinner. The island also offers several farmers' markets during peak season for guests who prefer to cook in our fully stocked kitchen.

Family Activities Beyond the Beach

While the beach is the main attraction, Hilton Head Island offers dozens of family-friendly activities. Explore the island by kayak or paddleboard, take a dolphin-watching cruise, visit the Sandbox Children's Museum, or enjoy go-karts and mini-golf along the south end. For nature enthusiasts, guided sea turtle walks during nesting season (June through August) are an unforgettable experience. Our beach house rental in Hilton Head SC serves as the perfect home base for all of these adventures.
